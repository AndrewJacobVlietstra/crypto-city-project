Crypto Security

Web Wallet

A Web Wallet is a web interface to the Dash blockchain. It is explicitly not an online wallet, meaning you maintain control over your private keys at all times. The private keys to your blockchain assets are generated and encrypted locally and stored on your computer ONLY. These keys never leave your computer.

Paper Wallet

Paper wallets are a form of cold wallet and essentially a bearer instrument – whoever holds them has access to both the Dash private key and therefore the address. Paper wallets are very secure and allow you to store your crypto in an off-the-grid storage.

Hardware Wallet

Hardware wallets are Security Devices that protect your Wallet from hackers and thieves. Hardware Wallets work with the wallet software on your computer by taking over the management of private key generation, private key storage, and transaction signing.

Desktop Wallet

Desktop Wallets provide full control of your cryptocurrency. You can verify the source code and binaries yourself. They also provide full verification without needing to trust if payments were sent or received and direct access to the p2p network.
